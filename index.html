Information about Munezero Isaac

Here is some information about Munezero Isaac

Munezero Isaac is a Senior 4 (S4) student currently studying Mathematics and Science at Ecole des Sciences de Musanze. He is a hardworking student who performs well in class. He is also a Christian and attends Eglise Presbyterienne au Rwanda.

He has participated in the AIMS Mathematics Competition and serves as a timekeeper at his school. In addition to his academic activities, Munezero Isaac is an artist. Although he is not widely known yet, he has around one thousand subscribers on YouTube. His only released song is titled “I Gave Her My Life”, and his stage name is Pro Max.

He prefers to stay focused on his studies rather than engaging in romantic relationships. Even during his O-Level studies at Groupe Scolaire Saint Joseph Kabgayi, he did not involve himself in serious relationships.

He enjoys playing football and supports Liverpool F.C (England), Real Madrid (Spain), and Rayon Sports (Rwanda). Among these, Liverpool F.C is his favorite team.

Currently, he is single. Although he sometimes thinks about finding a girlfriend, he has not made that decision yet. He is a helpful person and comes from a family with both parents.

He was born in the Western Province, Ngororero District, and currently lives in Muhanga District, Nyamabuye Sector, near Kabgayi in the Southern Province.

In Senior One, he was selected for the ISONGA Program and attended three camps:
- College du Christ Roi (Nyanza)
- Igihozo Saint Peter (Nyanza)
- TTC Save (Gisagara)

At TTC Save, he participated in a football competition against schools such as College de Gisenyi, Lycée de Kigali, Groupe Scolaire Kabare, and ES Nyamirama.

He played as a striker (number 9) and wore jersey number 21. He suffered a knee injury during a match at Imena Stadium in Huye. Despite the injury, he attempted to play in the semi-final at Huye Stadium but was later substituted.
